    Elemental Spells

    These spells hinge strongly on the use of the natural elements of the world. These are by far the most common types of spells used, however higher level wizards know how to combine them to make them much more formidable. What we have here are just a few of the simplest spells. All of which can also be combined in some way.

    Fire Spells

    Fireball

    Mana Cost - 2
    Mastery - Beginner

    The Fireball spell is fairly common. This is due to its extremely easy mastery level in addition to its low mana cost. For such a simple spell, it packs a good bit of heat. Literally.

    Sunflame

    Mana Cost - 30
    Mastery - Master

    This is a greatly renowned fire spell in the wizard world. Those who can cast it are of a different breed. This spell spawns a miniature Sun and slings it towards an enemy causing a great fiery explosion. Its destruction is formidable, with enough power to destroy entire forests.

    Water Spells

    Water Wall

    Mana Cost - 6
    Mastery - Intermediate

    Water wall is a common form of defense and offense. What makes it special is that unlike other forms of defense spells, Water Wall can be pushed forward at will. This can be useful to help control the battlefield. Additionally, this amount of water can be reused for other water spells.

    Typhoon

    Mana Cost - 45
    Mastery - Grand Master

    Summoning a swirling mass of water and wind, this spell is made for destruction. It is very uncommon that one can survive being sucked into such a spell. Its size alone requires a great mastery in the element of Water, let alone enough focus and strength to keep it going.

    Lightening Spells

    Lightening Bolt

    Mana Cost - 8
    Mastery - Experienced

    Summon a Bolt of lightening to shoot at your foes. This spell conjures up quickly, and fires in the blink of an eye. It damage is effective and hard to evade. This is a great middle-level spell.

    Super Conuctor

    Mana Cost - 35
    Mastery - Grand Master

    Create a thunderstorm to control at your will. Firing constant lighting bolts strong enough to destroy castle walls, this spell is not to be taken lightly. It is very uncommon to encounter this spell as it takes a mastery of multiple elements before you can perform Super Conductor.

    Earth Spells

    Boulder

    Mana Cost - 8
    Mastery - Intermediate

    Combine the Earth around you to create a boulder to use at your disposal. Boulders range in size depending on the Wizard who wields them. Some have even gotten so skilled they can create multiple at once.

    Tremor

    Mana Cost - 50
    Mastery - True Mage

    Use your own pure strength to shift the land beneath your feet. Cause earthquakes that terraform and destroy the earth one sits upon. Such a spell as destructive as this one is impossible to control. One can only cast and hope they don't die from the aftermath.

    Winds Spells

    Wind Disc

    Mana Cost - 5
    Mastery - Beginner

    Twirl the wind around you into discs that slice through the air like razors. The more skilled the Wizard, the more discs you can create. Though these can be dispelled quite easily, they are quick and ferocious. They are quite handy when thet are thrown in at random.

    Twister

    Mana Cost - 20
    Mastery - Expert

    Create a devastating twister that picks up and throws anything in its path. Such a spell is wondrous for crowd control and destroying enemy artillery. This spell can also be mixed with any flame spell to create a formidable combination.

    Healing Spells

    These spells are used to aid in ones healing, or in some cases completely reverse the damage done to ones self. In most cases a Wizard would specialize specifically in healing and medicines, however some Wizards learn a spell or two for their own safety.

    Pain Killer

    Mana Cost - 3
    Mastery - Beginner

    This spell merely stops pain for a set amount of time. It has no effect on extreme wounds.

    Lesser Heal

    Mana Cost - 8
    Mastery - Intermediate

    Lesser heal is great for healing small cuts and bruises. It also helps quicken the mitosis process for slightly deeper cuts. On average this spell takes a few minutes to work.

    Greater Heal

    Mana Cost - 20
    Mastery - Expert

    Deeper and more serious stabs or gashes along with fractures would be handled with the help of this spell. Any form of cuts that need stitches would be healed by this spell. Greater heal also takes significantly longer to execute.

    Weapon Enchantments

    Some Wizards like to rely on a Sword in addition to their staff. This would in turn provide them with another form of close quarters combat defense, and they would not have to rely as heavily on spell casting. To further increase their swords abilities, they will often enchant them. Enchantments last for a set time frame of usually a few minutes. They require a decent amount of focus, and since it lasts for a duration they take a substantial amount of mana.

    Flame Enchantment

    Mana Cost - 15
    Mastery - Expert

    Fairly straight forward this one is. It imbues your sword temporarily with flames, not only adding a burning trail to your swings, but the sword itself becomes incredibly hot.

    Ice Enchantment

    Mana Cost - 15
    Mastery - Expert

    Imbue your sword with icc, giving your sword the properties of the coldest of winters. Any contact with the blade causes immediate frostbite. After touching a surface for long enough, it will cool it so much to the point it can shatter.

    Poison Enchantment

    Mana Cost - 15
    Mastery - Expert

    Lining your blade with poison, any cuts given will immediately become infected with a debilitating poison.

    Light Enchantment

    Mana Cost - 15
    Mastery - Expert

    This causes your blade to glow brightly. Any Darkborn enemies will take more damage due to the light.

    Dark Enchantment

    Mana Cost - 15
    Mastery - Expert

    The exact opposite of the Light Enchantment. Enshrouds your blade in a thick, dark fog. Causes Lightborn creatures to take more damage.

    Magic Enchantment

    Mana Cost - 15
    Mastery - Expert

    This adds magical essence directly into your blade. It allows you to shoot arcs of pure magic straight from your blade when you swing your sword. Additionally, it also strengthens your armament.

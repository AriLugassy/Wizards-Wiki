Introduction To Wizards

Wizards have captured our imagination for centuries, appearing in folklore, literature, and popular media. These powerful beings are often depicted as wise and mysterious, wielding magic that shapes their world. For a fascinating overview of wizard mythology, you can explore this article.

Wizard Spells

Types of Spells

Wizard spells can be categorized into several types based on their effects:

  • Elemental Spells: Control the forces of nature, like fire, water, air, and earth.
  • Healing Spells: Restore health and cure ailments.
  • Enchantments: Bestow magical properties upon objects.

For an even deeper peek into the spells of the world, check out the spells page!

Spell Casting Techniques

Casting a spell is not as simple as learning, and casting. The art of a spell takes time to master, as do most things in this wonderful world. Any man can pick up a staff, but not every man can weild it. That is what will separate you from the rest. Certain spells require flicks and swooshes, perhaps a small phrase, and some need extreme focus. Learning the specifics, will make you an all around better spell caster.

Physical Strength

Most Wizards have very low physical strength, and little to no hand to hand combat knowledge. You may wonder why this is important, as do most. Proper physique will greatly improve your spell casting potency. Be prepared for close quarters combat, as not all your enemies will be Wizards. Strengthening yourself and your muscles will directly affect how strong your spells are. Just as you train to learn a spell, train to cast it as well. As the great Wizard of Light, Marshfinkle once said, “ A physically able Wizard, is a physically capable Wizard.”
